# LinkedIn — Founding Story

I was watching a Playing for Change video at 2am.

A street musician in New Orleans starts singing "Stand By Me." Then a guitarist in Barcelona joins in. A cellist in Moscow. Drummers in Congo. Singers in South Africa. Strangers on different continents, playing the same song — and it was the most beautiful thing I'd ever heard.

And I thought: what if we did this with problem-solving?

Not music. Problems. Real ones.

A farmer in Kenya who knows exactly why crops fail in laterite soil. A data scientist in Berlin who can build predictive models in her sleep. A designer in Lagos who makes complex things simple. None of them could solve the problem alone. Together, with AI as their shared instrument — they build something no institution ever would.

That's AI4Change.

After 13+ years building technology systems, I've seen what happens when the right tools reach the wrong people — and what happens when they don't reach the right ones. AI is crossing thresholds experts thought were a decade away. But the people who need it most are still the least likely to shape how it's used.

We're changing that. Open source. Problem-first. Global.

Every solution starts with a problem someone is brave enough to name. Every song starts with a single note someone is brave enough to play.

We just played ours.

Visit https://ai4change.org — submit a problem, join the community, or just come see what we're building.

The stage is open.
