Here's how AI4Change actually works 👇

Think of it as a flywheel — and it starts with YOU.

1️⃣ **PROBLEM** — Someone names a real problem. Not a tech problem — a human one. Like: "Smallholder farmers lose 30% of crops to disease they can't identify early enough."

2️⃣ **SIGNAL** — The community rallies. People vote, discuss, connect their expertise. "I have agronomic data." "I build image classifiers." "I design mobile interfaces."

3️⃣ **BUILD** — A global team forms. AI is the instrument. They build together, across time zones, in the open.

4️⃣ **SHIP** — Everything ships open source. Not a demo. A real tool that real people can use, fork, and improve 🚀

5️⃣ **STORY** — The journey gets documented. Who built it. Why it matters. Because storytelling shows the next person: "Someone like me did this."

6️⃣ **REPEAT** — Each story inspires the next problem. The flywheel spins faster 🔄

This is how a farmer, a data scientist, and a designer on three different continents end up building something no one could build alone.
