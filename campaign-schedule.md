## Posting Best Practices

- **LinkedIn:** 08:00-10:00 AWST (catches AU morning + EU/Africa afternoon + Americas late night)
- **X/Twitter:** 09:00-15:00 AWST (overlaps with multiple global audiences)
- **Instagram:** 12:00-18:00 AWST (visual content performs better midday-evening)
- **Reddit:** 08:00-14:00 AWST (catches US evening and EU morning traffic)
- **Hacker News:** 12:00 AWST (Tuesday-Thursday are highest-traffic days)
- **Product Hunt:** Launch at midnight PT (16:00 AWST) for a full voting day
- **Engage within 1 hour** of posting — early engagement signals boost algorithmic reach
- **Cross-link** between platforms: reference the LinkedIn post in X, mention the IG visual in LinkedIn
- **Pin** the launch thread on X and the founding story on LinkedIn for the full week

- [ ] No aggressive language (shift/evolution instead of war/crisis)
- [ ] Tone is educational, inspiring, constructive
- [ ] Each X tweet is under 280 characters
- [ ] LinkedIn posts are 1000-1800 characters
- [ ] Instagram captions have 10-15 hashtags
- [ ] CTAs are clear and point to the website
