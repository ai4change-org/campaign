# AI4Change — Social Media Launch Campaign Brief

## What Is AI4Change?
A global open-source initiative inspired by Playing for Change. Instead of musicians creating songs across borders, AI4Change connects problem-solvers worldwide who use AI as their instrument to solve real-world problems. A farmer in Kenya + a data scientist in Berlin + a designer in Lagos = an open-source solution no one could build alone.

**Tagline:** "AI as the Great Equalizer"
**Website:** https://ai4change-org.github.io
**GitHub:** https://github.com/ai4change-org
**Founder:** Valter Silva (13+ years in tech, cloud architect, Perth Australia, Brazilian origin)

## The Playing for Change Parallel
- PFC: Musicians from around the world, each playing their instrument, mixed into one song
- AI4Change: Problem-solvers from around the world, each using AI as their instrument, building one open-source solution
- PFC: Street musician = Grammy winner
- AI4Change: Farmer = Fortune 500 analyst

## Key Messaging
- "Every solution starts with a problem. Every song starts with a single note."
- "When you give people the same instrument, the distance between them disappears."
- AI is crossing thresholds experts thought were a decade away
- The people who need AI most are least likely to shape how it's used
- We start with the PROBLEM, not the technology
- Open source is non-negotiable
- Storytelling is infrastructure

## Content Rules (MUST FOLLOW)
- NEVER mention AWS by name
- NEVER mention customer names (BHP, ANZ, Woodside, etc.)
- NEVER use aggressive words (war, crisis, destroyed, devastating)
- USE constructive framing (shift, evolution, opportunity, transformation)
- Tone: Educational, inspiring, constructive, optimistic
- Frame as opportunities, not threats

## Campaign Structure — Create ALL of these files:

### 1. linkedin-founding-story.md
The big LinkedIn announcement. Valter's personal story of watching Playing for Change and having the "what if" moment. 1200-1800 chars. Professional but vulnerable. End with CTA to visit the site and submit problems. Include 5-7 hashtags.

### 2. linkedin-manifesto.md
A shorter LinkedIn post distilling the manifesto — the "why now" argument. Why AI is the great equalizer RIGHT NOW, not in 5 years. 1000-1500 chars. 5-7 hashtags.

### 3. linkedin-how-it-works.md
LinkedIn post explaining the flywheel: Problems → Community Signal → Build → Ship → Story → More Problems. Make it concrete with examples. 1000-1500 chars. 5-7 hashtags.

### 4. x-thread-launch.md
An X/Twitter thread (8-10 tweets). The launch announcement. Punchy, each tweet <280 chars. First tweet is the hook. Last tweet is the CTA with link.

### 5. x-manifesto-tweet.md
Single killer tweet (<280 chars) that captures the entire AI4Change vision in one shot.

### 6. x-pfc-parallel.md
Single tweet or mini-thread (3-4 tweets) about the Playing for Change parallel. Visual and emotional.

### 7. ig-launch-caption.md
Instagram launch caption. Visual storytelling tone. Authentic, inspiring. 10-15 hashtags. Include emoji naturally.

### 8. ig-manifesto-caption.md
Instagram caption for the manifesto/mission visual. Motivational angle. 10-15 hashtags.

### 9. ig-how-it-works-caption.md
Instagram caption for the flywheel/how-it-works visual. Educational but accessible. 10-15 hashtags.

### 10. campaign-schedule.md
A posting schedule: which post goes where, in what order, over the first 7 days. Include timing recommendations (AWST timezone). Structure as a clear calendar.

Write compelling, authentic content. This is a MOVEMENT launch, not a product announcement. Make people FEEL something.
